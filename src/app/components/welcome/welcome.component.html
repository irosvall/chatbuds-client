Welcome to ChatBuds!

ChatBuds gives an opportunity to meet unexpected friends all over the world!

To get chatting buddies you simply join a line and get matched with an other user in the line. Then you get redirected to a private chat with this user were you can chat and get to know each other. If you feel like becoming friends then you can send away a friend request and then you can always chat with them. If it doesn't go good then you can at any point choose to find a new chatting buddy and the process starts over.

No conversations in ChatBuds are stored persitantly, so you can be assured of your own privacy.
